### Light Theme

## Accessibility

### Dark Theme
- Contrast Ratio: 15:1 (AAA)
- Good for: Light sensitivity
- Challenge: Can be harder to read in bright light

### Light Theme
- Contrast Ratio: 16:1 (AAA)
- Good for: General readability
- Challenge: Can cause glare in dark rooms
